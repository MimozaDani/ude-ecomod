Synchronous Zoom meetings

I will offer completely optional Zoom meetings. See the Communication section for some more details. You can find the Zoom link on Slack. The current plan is to have one Zoom meeting per module on Thursdays, alternating between 3pm (odd modules) and 8am (even modules). (That means, most of the time, one meeting per week, unless a module is longer than a week, as it is for the first one.)

Hopefully, one of those two times works for everyone, allowing people to join at least every other time if they want. If you already know that neither times work for you, and you are keen on joining the Zoom meetings, let me know and we can adjust.

The first Zoom session will be on Thursday, 8/19 at 3pm ET.
